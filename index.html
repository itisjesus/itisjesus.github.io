Dr. Michel Kenmogne

Executive Director of SIL International

Here are Dr. Kenmogne's important achievements so far:

  • 1965 - Born in Baham, Cameroon
  • 1987 - Earns his bachelor's degree in French Studies at University of Yaoundé, Cameroon
  • 1989 - Earns his master's degree in African Linguistics at University of Yaoundé, Cameroon
  • 1993 - Marries Laure Angele
  • 1994 - Birth of his first daughter, Prisca
  • 1997 - Birth of his second daughter, Blessing
  • 1999 - Birth of his third daughter, Glory
  • 2001 - Earns his Ph.D. in African Linguistics at University of Buea, Cameroon
  • From 2000 to 2005 - Served as CABTAL's Director of Language Programs
  • 2002 - Birth of his fourth child, Gaddiel
  • 2004 - Birth of his fifth child (fourth daughter), Abiele
  • From 2004 to 2011 - Served as General Director of CABTAL (Cameroon Association for Bible Translation and Literacy)
  • From 2012 to 2015 - Served as Associate Director of Wycliffe Global Alliance for Francophone Africa
  • From 2016 until now - Serves as Executive Director of SIL International
